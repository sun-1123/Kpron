CONTRIBUTE TO KPRON
IN THE
Kusama slot auction
Kpron Network will join the Kusama slot auction when the second slot auction opens. Kpron will bid for an 8-period slot, which means Kpron will lease the slot for 6 x 8 = 48 weeks.
1KSM = 100APN
If Kpron wins, Every KSM that supports Kpron in the Kusama slot auction through the Crowdloan will be entitled to 100 APN as rewards.
15,000,000 APN Rewards
Up to 15,000,000 APN are available as rewards in the Kusama auction (100:1 on up to 150,000 KSM).
Prompt Payment
When Kpron wins a slot and runs as parachain, 34% of the APN rewards vest to contributor addresses immediately, with 66% vesting monthly over 11 months.
Referral Rewards
When Crowdloan Dapp participants invite others who contribute, each will receive an additional reward: an extra 0.5% on the additional contribution.
